The School

Academic excellence

The school has established itself as an academic giant, consistently performing strongly since the start of the 8-4-4 system.

Board of management

The board, led by the chair Sam Ncheeri, provides strategic leadership and is the backbone of the school administration.

The PTA

The Parents Association, led by Prof. Gideon Maina (Vice Chancellor, Pioneer University), brings together parents in support of the school.

Students & admission

The school admits students who have excelled in primary schools across the country. Being a public school, placement is done by the Ministry of Education through merit and choice. A student selects the school in their candidate class and must achieve the required entry score.

Grade 10 admission

Information about who is admitted in Grade 10, and the criteria used, can be obtained from the school office and official admission guidelines.

Societies & Clubs

Activities outside the academics that students are involved in.

ICT Club

Brings together students who have shown interest in computers and guides them to deepen their understanding of ICT.

St. John's Ambulance

Trains students to save lives, especially in the first critical minutes before medics arrive.

Model United Nations

Helps students understand current affairs, people and the world through debate, research and diplomacy.
